Refund Policy

Last updated: {{ "2025-01-15" }}

1. Service Booking Refunds

Refund eligibility for service bookings:

  • Before Service Starts: Full refund if cancelled 2+ hours before scheduled time
  • Service Not Started: Full refund if service provider fails to arrive
  • Incomplete Service: Partial refund based on work completed and issue assessment
  • Unsatisfactory Service: Refund considered on case-by-case basis after review
  • No Refund: If service is completed satisfactorily or cancelled less than 2 hours before appointment

2. Product Order Refunds

Refund policy for shop orders:

  • Order Cancellation: Full refund if cancelled before order is processed
  • Return Policy: 7-day return window for defective or damaged products
  • Wrong Item: Full refund or replacement for incorrectly delivered items
  • Delivery Charges: Non-refundable unless order is cancelled or undelivered due to shop error
  • Custom Orders: Non-refundable unless product is defective

3. Wallet Refunds

Refund policy for wallet credits:

  • Razorpay Top-ups: Wallet top-ups are non-refundable but can be used for future services
  • Unused Credits: Credits remain in wallet and can be used at any time
  • Account Closure: Refund of unused credits may be processed upon account closure (subject to review)
  • Fraudulent Transactions: Full refund for unauthorized transactions after investigation

4. Provider Deposit Refunds

Refund policy for provider deposits:

  • Account Closure: Refund of remaining deposit balance (minus outstanding commissions) upon provider account closure
  • Commission Deductions: Non-refundable - commissions deducted for cash payments are final
  • Minimum Balance: ₹500 minimum deposit must be maintained - cannot be withdrawn while account is active

5. Refund Processing Time

  • Payment Gateway: 5-7 business days for Razorpay refunds
  • Wallet Credits: Instant refund to wallet balance
  • Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days for bank account refunds
  • Provider Deposits: 10-15 business days after account closure approval

6. Refund Method

Refunds are processed through the original payment method:

  • Razorpay payments → Refunded to original payment method
  • Wallet payments → Refunded to wallet balance
  • Cash payments → Credit to wallet or bank transfer (for service bookings)

7. Non-Refundable Items

  • Completed services that were satisfactory
  • Delivery charges (unless undelivered due to shop/provider error)
  • Commission fees deducted from provider deposits
  • Platform service fees
  • Custom or personalized products (unless defective)

8. How to Request a Refund

  1. Log in to your Hofix account
  2. Navigate to "My Orders" or "Bookings"
  3. Select the order/booking you want to refund
  4. Click "Request Refund"
  5. Provide reason for refund request
  6. Submit request - our team will review within 24-48 hours

9. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  • Contact our support team with order details
  • Provide evidence (photos, messages, receipts)
  • Our team will review and provide a resolution
  • Final decisions will be communicated via email
